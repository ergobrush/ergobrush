Ergonomic Design

Ergobrush is a gimbal style device providing three axis of rotation that allows one to go through the steps and positions of brushing their teeth without having to actually move their hand.

Built with Arduino

Every Ergobrush is built utilizing Arduino technology, the industry's leading manufacturer of single-board microcontrollers for digital devices.

Colaboration

In colaboration with the University of Ottawa's Centre for Entrepreneurship and Engineering Design (CEED), our team was provided with the required resources enabling us to develop Ergobrush.

Built with compassion

Ergobrush was designed for our client, a patient at the St. Vincent long term care Hospital in Ottawa, Ontario. Due to the limited hand mobility our client experiences, we developed a device that would provide aide and independence while she brushed her teeth.
